Fluffy Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Use your microwave to make light and fluffy scrambled eggs for a quick and easy breakfast to start your day. Follow the technique in this recipe for perfect results every time.

Your microwave is the key to irresistibly fluffy scrambled eggs that are ready in mere minutes. Don't believe us? Just try this top-rated recipe for microwave eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Break, season and whisk.
  3. Microwave and stir.
  4. Repeat until consistent.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
